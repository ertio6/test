Spanish Potato Omelet

or tortilla de patatas, consisting of an rustic omelette
made with eggs and potato, fried in oil. It is the classic

Information: Ready in 45 minutes / 6 servings / 252 cal

List of ingredients

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 pound potatoes, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 eggs

Directions

  1. In a large frying pan or skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Sprinkle potatoes lightly with salt and pepper. Cook until golden brown and crisp.
  2. Once the potatoes are golden, stir in the onions. Cook, stirring occasionally, until onions soften and begin to brown
  3. Meanwhile, beat eggs together with salt and pepper. Pour eggs into pan and stir gently to combine. Reduce heat to low and cook until eggs begin to brown on the bottom.
  4. Loosen bottom of omelet with a spatula, invert a large plate over the pan, and carefully turn the omelet out onto it. Slide the omelet back into the pan with the uncooked side down. Cook until eggs are set. Garnish omelet for example with tomato and green onion and serve warm
